How to create a Dropshipping Business in 2025

What is dropshipping?

Dropshipping is where you become a middle man, reselling cheap items for a higher price.

How YOU can dropship

  1. Decide whether you want to make a convincing product or not
  2. Find items to dropship-- this can be done by searching websites such as AliBaba for cheap bargain
  3. Order items to your property
  4. Repackage-- this can use a full new box, or can be a design over the original
  5. If you want to cut corners, you can always keep the same packaging.
  6. Make an account for a tiktok shop page or Facebook Marketplace
  7. Create a page for the item-- It can be a good idea to use AI to make videos or images
  8. You can make fake comments and reviews on account-- can be a difficult process but will bring more consumers or cover up for a poor product
  9. Ship the item to any buyers, either directly from the business or from where you packaged them.
