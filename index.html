How to create a Dropshipping Business in 2025

What is dropshipping?

Before considering dropshipping as a business idea you may use, you must first understand what it is. Dropshipping is an online business method where you sell products to customers without ever making and holding the items yourself. You act as a middleman to re-sell products originally produced by another company, and make profit off of the margin between what you bought it for and what you re sold it for. It can be a great first business, as there is no real cost of entry before you start selling items.

Business Model Entry Cost ($) Costs
Dropshipping 0 to 3000 Cost of domain and website, sampling products, advertising. Little fluctuation.
E-Commerce 500 to 5000 Cost of website, testing products, initial small inventory. Warehousing, inventory management and more. Can fluctuate greatly.
Physical Retail 10,000+ Similar to E-Commerce, but costs associated to the physical property and larger initial inventory drive up costs. High fluctuation.

How YOU can dropship

  1. Decide whether you want to make a convincing product or not
  2. Find items to dropship-- this can be done by searching websites such as AliBaba for cheap items
  3. If you want to make a more convincing product, order the items to your property.
  4. Repackage-- this can use a full new box, or can be a design over the original
  5. If you want to cut corners, you can always keep the same packaging and send the product directly to buyers.
  6. Make an account for a tiktok shop page or Facebook Marketplace
  7. Create a page for the item-- It can be a good idea to use AI to make videos or images, or simply copy the page you bought it from
  8. Choose pricing for the item. You can mark it up multiple times its value, but it is a good idea to use intuition as higher prices will drive away customers
  9. You can advertise the product, or just wait to get purchases.
  10. Ship the item to any buyers, either directly from the business or from where you packaged them.
